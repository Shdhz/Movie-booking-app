Gampang Cuan


In the midst of the family's ongoing financial difficulties, Sultan, the first child who has been living in Jakarta for years and always claims to be successful to his family in the village, is confused by the news that his late father left an inheritance in the form of a debt of 300 million. This debt must be paid within 3 months, or their house in the village will be confiscated. Unfortunately, this news came at the same time as the arrival of Bilqis, his younger sister, who learned that all of the Sultan's successes were ephemeral. The problem is, Mamah, their mother, has heart disease and gets stressed easily. So these brothers and sisters have to work together to find money to pay off debts. Sultan tries all possible jobs, while Bilqis almost falls into being a (almost) plus-plus massage therapist.
